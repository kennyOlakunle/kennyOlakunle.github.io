## Overview

This project focused on clarity, trust, and conversion. The site needed to quickly explain what the business offers, look credible on first impression, and remain easy to use across mobile and desktop.

## Problem

Service businesses often lose visitors because their websites bury the offer, overload the layout, or fail to guide people toward a simple next step. The goal here was to create a cleaner front door for the business.

## What I built

- A landing page structure centered around services, trust signals, and contact actions
- Responsive sections that keep the experience strong on smaller screens
- Visual hierarchy for transport and automotive solution messaging
- A presentation style aimed at helping visitors understand the offer within seconds

## Why it matters

Even when the tech stack is simple, the product thinking matters. This build demonstrates how frontend work supports business outcomes through layout, readability, and conversion flow rather than just styling.
